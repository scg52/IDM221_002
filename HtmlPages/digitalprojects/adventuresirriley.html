The Adventure of *Almost* Sir Riley

The Adventure of Almost Sir Riley was the first video game I worked on. I was the project manager and my team was Caeleb Nasof, Jake Carfagno, Adrien Rosebrook, Javier Figueroa, and Kevin Riemensperger. It was a 2D platformer where you play as a dog-knight named sir Riley and you fight an army of invading gnomes. The final boss was Gertrude the Goblin Gnome. This game placed third in the 2014 Drexel University Computing Academy.

Stephen Gross 2016
